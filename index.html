Blur-inspired local split-screen combat racer

Neon Rush Duel

A fast two-player duel built for phones and computers: split screen, racing lines, boost pads, pulse shots, and same-device multiplayer.

Desktop

Player 1 steers with A / D, Player 2 steers with Left / Right Arrow, and shooting happens with the left mouse button.

Mobile

Each player gets their own left, right, and fire controls inside their screen half.

This demo uses original visuals and track art inspired by arcade combat racers. It does not copy Blur's exact maps or assets.
